WHO WE ARE
Cryon's CLothing Store is dedicated to delivering an exclusive collection of the most relevant brands and styles to a community of inspired youths. Through partnerships with brands such as adidas, Brandy Melville, Essentials Fear of God, our own brands, and many more, we offer a unique range of product that all youth can express themselves through.
Community Cryon believes in and understands the importance of using our voice, platform, and resources to inspire and bring about positive development in our local and global communities. Through meaningful partnerships and engagement, our goal is to inspire and positively impact the people around us, near and far.
Our People Cryon's HQ comprises 53% of associates that identify as POC or of minority descent. With strategies in place to increase this number, we strive to create and maintain a workforce that is both diverse and inclusive.

Philanthropy & Outreach Cryon Cares was started in 2020 to support and partner with organizations that align with the company's values of good mental health, diversity, and equality. We commit to continuously learning and taking action through philanthropic initiatives and outreach.
CryonTalks Join us bi-weekly on Instagram Live as we discuss topics such as diversity and inclusion, mental health, stress relief, and more. With help from guest speakers, our goal is to encourage the dialogue and understanding of important societal topics
Cryon Cares Proceeds from our CryonCares merchandise supports our charitable partners: Born This Way Foundation, Girl Up, STEM To The Future, and NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, Inc. (LDF)
Black Lives Matter Cryon commits to addressing and preventing systemic racism in our organization and the fashion industry through active learning, assessment, and action.
