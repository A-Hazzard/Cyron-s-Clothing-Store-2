OUR SHIPPING POLICY

How It Works

We offer free standard shipping globally, Orders are usually shipped within 1-3 business days and transit takes 1-7 business days. Pre-order items will be shipped upon their arrival at our offices. Estimated pre-order ship dates are displayed on each product page and while we aim to ship by the estimated date, there may be unforeseen production delays by the manufacturer. If there are multiple pre-order items in an order, we may in certain cases, ship the order once all items have arrived at our offices.
